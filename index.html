Wearable Arts Runway Show

What is the Runway Show?

Runway is part club, part annual event where students create "wearable art" to be worn down the runway. Students use non-traditional, recycled materials to build amazing garments and display their creativity. See example garments in next tab.

Runway Show Location and dates

The Wearable Arts Runway Show will be held on Saturday, January 20th. The show will have two times, 2-4pm, 7-9pm and be held in the Greenway Rise Campus Cafeteria. Tickets will be made aviable closer to the date.
